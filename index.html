Introduction to Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players each on a field with a pitch at the centre. The pitch is 22 yards long and 10 feet wide, with wickets at each end consisting of three stumps and two bails.

The game is played in innings, where one team bats and the other fields. The batting team tries to score runs, while the fielding team tries to get the batsmen out.

Test Cricket

The longest format, played over 5 days, testing endurance and skill.

ODI Cricket

One Day Internationals, 50 overs each, fast-paced and exciting.

T20 Cricket

Twenty20, quick format with 20 overs, maximum entertainment.
